In 2012, I got my Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Oporto in Portugal, where I specialized in Automation, Instrumentation, and Control Systems in order to match my interests in electronics and software with my earlier interests in machinery and production.

My career has been marked by flexibility and a broad range of skill sets, starting with the development of machines in the metalworking industry, followed by the automation of numerous products, the development of software for additive manufacturing, the testing of aerospace, industrial robotics, IIoT, and the digital transformation.

Currently, my major areas of interest are; digital transformation (particularly in the industrial sector), data analytics, the application of AI and virtual reality, but I'm always keen to put my skill set to full use and, most importantly, to be a crucial member of any team or organisation with whom I work.
